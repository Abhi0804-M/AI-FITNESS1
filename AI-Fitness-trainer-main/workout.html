Pull-Up

A pull-up is an upper-body strength exercise.

Push-Up

It is a conditioning exercise performed in a prone position.

Sit-Up

The sit-up is an abdominal endurance training exercise.

Walking Exercise

Walking is a simple and effective form of exercise.

Squat

A squat is a strength exercise in which the trainee lowers their hips.

Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a full-body workout that engages multiple muscle groups.

Lunges

Lunges are a lower-body exercise that targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings.

Leg Raises

Leg raises target the lower abdominal muscles.

Burpees

Burpees are a full-body exercise that combines a squat, push-up, and jump.
